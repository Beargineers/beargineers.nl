1. **Discovery** — explore new skills and ideas through hands-on engineering, programming, and problem-solving.
2. **Innovation** — iterative design and testing; embrace failure to improve robot performance and team efficiency.
3. **Impact** — apply what we learn to uplift our school/community through STEM outreach and role modeling.
4. **Inclusion** — respectful, welcoming environment where all perspectives are valued and every member contributes.
5. **Teamwork** — collaboration across engineering, programming, outreach, and strategy with open communication and shared responsibility.
6. **Fun** — celebrate learning and competition; keep a positive, enthusiastic culture.
