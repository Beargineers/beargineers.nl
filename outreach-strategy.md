### Strategic objective
Sustainability through a strong talent pipeline from middle school into FTC.

### Junior Bear-League
- Turned weekly club into two rookie teams using old chassis and spare parts.
- Simulated season: seniors acted as GDC, rookies designed, built, and scrimmaged to experience FIRST before joining.
- Result: 100% of Junior League members committed to FTC next year.

### Recruiting non-technical mentors
- Pitched STEAM to Arts Department; recruited Mr. Van den Hoven (no prior robotics) to mentor portfolio design with biweekly critiques.
